Our Cleaning Services

At Keep Clean With Wealth we deliver reliable, affordable, and professional cleaning tailored to your needs.

Whether it’s your home, office, or a one-time deep clean, we’ve got you covered.

Residential Cleaning

  • Regular house cleaning (dusting, sweeping, vacuuming, mopping)
  • Kitchen and bathroom sanitization
  • Bedroom and living areas refreshed
  • Laundry & ironing (on request)

& Commercial Cleaning

  • Desk and workstation cleaning
  • Floor care (vacuuming, mopping, polishing)
  • Common area cleaning (meeting rooms, kitchens, breakrooms)
  • Waste disposal and sanitization

Deep Cleaning & One-Time Services

  • Move-in / Move-out cleaning
  • End-of-tenancy cleaning
  • Post-renovation cleaning
  • Oven, fridge, and appliance deep cleaning
  • Carpet & upholstery care

Specialty Cleaning

  • Eco-friendly “green” cleaning (non-toxic products)
  • Short-let / Airbnb property cleaning
  • Event & party clean-up (before & after)
  • Garden/patio sweeping and tidying
  • Flexible scheduling (daily, weekly, monthly, or one-off)
  • Custom packages tailored to your needs
  • Trained and reliable cleaners you can trust
